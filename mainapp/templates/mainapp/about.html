“At EcoPath, we believe roads aren’t just made of materials, they’re made of purpose. Behind every road we build is a dream: a farmer who wants to reach the market on time, a child who needs a safe walk to school, a village that deserves to feel connected to the world.”

About Ecopath

Ever since EcoPath came into existence on 22nd April 2024, we've always asked ourselves, why should building roads come at the cost of the planet? That's where our mission comes from to create roads that connect people, uplift communities, and care for the earth, all at the same time. This isn't just a business for us. It's a commitment to prove that progress doesn't need to be polluting, and that the best roads are the ones that leave no one, and nothing, behind.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to decarbonize the construction industry and make green construction mainstream and affordable. We want to achieve a 10 million ton reduction in CO2 emissions by 2030. Our vision is to build reliable road connectivity for 50 of the most underdeveloped villages, driving progress as part of our social responsibility.

Achievements & Collaboration

  • Delivered carbon-neutral paver installations.
  • Supplied to army workshops and municipalities.
  • Replaced cement in pilot roads, drains, and blocks.

We collaborate with:

  • Urban local bodies (ULBs)
  • Real estate developers
  • Smart city missions
  • Green building certification bodies (IGBC, GRIHA)
